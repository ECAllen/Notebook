Ladder up

Netflix example:
Walmart movies -> DVDs -> streaming -> content

Dont look at the business you want to build but rather the business you can start today

suggested ladder:
rung 1 - minimum viable audience as opposed to mvp, let audience dictate mvp
rung 2 - build product get to 6 figures
rung 3 - build real business to get to 7 figures

rung 1 (MVA)
  • 3 to 6 months to get 2,000 email subs, you can use mailchimp, drip.co ...
  • if you cant get people to follow your blog then probably wont have marketing chops to to build a product
  • howto chooose audience:
    • go with your passion
    • would you be comfortable sharing with friends and family
    • do you use it
    • Kevin Kelly essay 1,000 true fans
  • 2,000 subscribers is good # bc can be potentially be 2$ per subscriber per month
  • dont worry about selling product yet except maybe consulting
  • this phase to develope marketing in a way to get audience to comment and subscribe
  • work on messaging
  • use this phase as research and pain points
  • most people overestimate how hard it is to get someone to subscribe but underestimate how easy it is to get someone to buy product

rung 2
  • prereq MVA 2k subs
  • within 2 months make 6 figures of annual revenue ~8-9k/mo
  • rule of 5 ones/limit to:
    • 1 product
    • 1 conversion mechanism
    • 1 traffic source ex. SEO, traffic, pay per click
    • 1 year to find right combo of above
    • 1 year to get to 7 figures
    • 1 product = one package/offer
  • recommend weekly webinar and measure conversion rate
  • provide tremendous value so that it is not icky to pitch sale
  • if you have MVA they will come to you with ideas
  • 80/20 rule, what is providing most results
  • 5 commandments:
    • only one business
    • only have one offer
    • no more than 1 day per month on product creation (instead focus on marketing)
    • no more than 1 day per month on content marketing
    • find beauty in depth rather and breadth
  • use the conversion mechanism that you normally use otherwise (blogs etc...)
  • basic steps recap:
    • create product
    • create package (sales tiers etc...)
    • create conversion process
    • get reliable traffic source
rung 3

example timeline: 3 months rung 1, 12 months to get to rung 2 = 25 months
